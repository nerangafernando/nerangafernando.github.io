I am a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics  at <a href="https://www.knox.edu"> Knox College </a> in Galesburg, Illinois. 

I was born in Negombo in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lanka"> Sri Lanka </a> and raised in Ekala, which is a small town in the west coast of Sri Lanka. I completed my elementary education at <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newstead_Girls_College"> Newstead Girls College </a> in Negombo and then went to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maris_Stella_College"> Maris Stella College </a> in Negombo to complete my middle school education and high school education. Having earned my B.Sc. (special) degree in Mathematics at the <a href="http://www.kln.ac.lk/"> University of Kelaniya </a> in Sri Lanka, I moved to Florida to complete my Master's degree and Doctoral degree in Mathematics at the <a href="https://www.usf.edu/"> University of South Florida </a>. I completed my Ph.D. in Spring 2013 at the University of South Florida under the supervision of <a href="http://math.usf.edu/faculty/xdohou/"> Professor Xiang-dong Hou </a> with a dissertation titled <i>A study of permutation polynom ials over finite fields</i>. I moved to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boston"> Boston </a> in August, 2013 to be a Lecturer (this designation was renamed as Assistant Teaching Professor in 2018) in the Department of Mathematics at Northeastern University, and I was there until June, 2019. In July 2019, I moved to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pittsburgh"> Pittsburgh </a> to join Carnegie Mellon University for a year. I took a break in the academic year 2020/2021. I spent sometime in Indiana and came back to Pittsburgh. In July 2021, I moved to  <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Worcester,_Massachusetts"> Worcester </a>  to join the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at College of the Holy Cross as a Visiting Assistant Professor. In August 2025, I joined the Department of Mathematics at Knox College in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galesburg,_Illinois"> Galesburg</a>, Illinois.
